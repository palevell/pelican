Evaluating Pelican Themes

After speeding through several Pelican themes, I have noticed a few things…

  • Many of the themes don’t seem to work, anymore (UPDATE: most of them need the i18n_subsites plugin)
  • Themes I like:
    • pelican-twitchy
    • bold
    • Just Read
    • Genus
    • bricks
    • Atilla
    • materialistic (very much!)
  • Boostrap colour schemes that are worth exploring:
    • simplex
  • Themes I like, but need improvement:
    • Pelican Blue
    • Monospace
    • Taman
    • voidy-bootstrap
    • nikhil-theme
    • bootstrap
  • Themes with tweet display potential:
    • New Bootstrap2
  • Incomplete themes, can be deleted:
    • lazystrap
    • niu-x2
  • Themes with images:
    • SoMA
    • Taman
    • Atilla
